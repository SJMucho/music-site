About

Sarah Mucho is a Brooklyn-based, award winning singer, musician, songwriter and cabaret artist. A unique and diverse vocalist with musical projects ranging from folk, jazz, Americana to rock, soul and blues, Sarah also plays the guitar, harmonica and several percussive instruments. She has performed hundreds of shows in the NY Metropolitan/Tri-State area as well as Greece, Northern Ireland and England. You can catch her live at Freddy’s bar on the 2nd Sunday of each month, 5-8pm.
